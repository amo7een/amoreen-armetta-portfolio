FILED UNDER — Billing, Pricing & Packaging

Building a system, not just an article

Some documentation problems aren't solved by writing better sentences — they're solved by changing where the sentences live.

PROBLEM

Plan, pricing, and permissions details for every feature were duplicated across dozens of Help Center articles. Every pricing or packaging change meant hunting down and editing each one individually — with a real risk of articles falling out of sync.

APPROACH

Designed a custom CSS system for a new "Feature Availability and Limits" article format, so plan, pricing, and permissions details for each feature group are defined once and referenced everywhere they're needed.

RESULT

Pricing and packaging updates now require one edit instead of dozens. The system removed an entire category of content drift — and the risk that shipped with it.

In-app guidance (Pendo)

Partnered with Product Growth and Marketing on in-app messaging that reduced customer frustration around billing and permissions policy — catching confusion at the moment it happens, before it becomes a support ticket.
